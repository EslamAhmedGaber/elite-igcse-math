Parent & Student FAQ

Quick answers before you message.

Clear answers make the first step easier, especially for parents comparing online tutors.

Is the website free for students?

Yes. Students can use the classified bank, solutions, topic roadmap, notes, downloads, and study plan without creating an account.

What is included in the free first class?

Dr Eslam checks the student's level, identifies weak topics, explains one or two exam-style questions, and suggests the best study route.

Are lessons online or in person?

The course is 100% online by Zoom. Sessions are recorded so students can rewatch difficult questions later.

Do you teach in English or Arabic?

Lessons are mixed English and Arabic. Exam wording and mathematical notation are kept in English, with Arabic explanation when it helps understanding.

How is this different from random past-paper practice?

The questions are classified by topic, linked to solutions, and connected to notes and a study plan. Students know exactly what to practise next.

Can parents track progress?

Students can mark questions as solved on the Practice page and print a study plan. Enrolled students also receive teacher guidance on weak topics.

Have questions? Just message Dr Eslam.

Tell him your year, your target exam session, and any topic you find hardest. He replies personally on WhatsApp.
