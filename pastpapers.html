Linear · 4MA1

Linear (single tier)

Two papers - P1H and P2H - sat in the same exam session. This is the spec most international schools enter their students for. HR variants (e.g. P1HR) are alternate papers used in the same session.

Modular · 4WM

Modular (split into units)

Two units assessed separately - 4WM1H (Unit 1) and 4WM2H (Unit 2). Students can sit each unit in different sessions and combine the results. R variants are alternate papers.
